Accommodations:


A limited number of rooms at a special conference rate of $149 have been reserved at the Millennium Harvest House. The hotel is at walking distance (5min) from the conference venue, by Boulder Creek, with a pool and tennis courts.

To book your room email: boulder.reservations@millenniumhotels.com
or call the hotel toll free at (800) 545-6285 and refer to the group code: 1505WIRELE
